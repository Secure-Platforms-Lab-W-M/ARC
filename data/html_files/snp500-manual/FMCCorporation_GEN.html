With your consent (where required by applicable law), we may collect and use different types of data from and about you, including Personal Data and non-personal details. This data includes your:

  • Measure traffic patterns, including which areas of our Website users visit, and visiting patterns in the aggregate; 

  • Understand how our users’ habits are similar or different from one another; and 

  • Recognize you when you return to our Website. 

We use this research so that we can make each new experience on our Website a better one.  You may also unsubscribe by following the links within any electronic marketing materials we, third-party ad companies and ad networks send to you 

You may change your cookie settings on your internet browser or delete our cookies at any time, except for essential cookies that are necessary to ensure basic Website functions, such as securing the Website and directing user traffic on the Website. You may also refuse to accept browser cookies by activating the appropriate setting on your browser or clicking “Decline” on the cookie banner on the Website. However, please note that if you disable cookies, all or part(s) of our Website may become inaccessible and certain features may not work correctly. Unless you adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or decline cookies on our cookie banner, our system will issue them. For detailed information on the cookies we use, the purposes for which we use them, and how you can exercise your choices regarding our use of your cookies, see our Cookie Policy.  

Our Website pages and e-mails may contain web beacons (small transparent embedded images or objects, also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit us, for example, to count website page visitors or email readers, or to compile other similar statistics such as recording Website content popularity or verifying system and server integrity.

We will consider your complaint and determine whether it requires further investigation. We will notify you of the outcome of this investigation and any subsequent internal investigation.  

If you remain unsatisfied with the way in which we have handled a privacy issue, you may approach an independent advisor or contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (www.oaic.gov.au) for guidance on alternative courses of action which may be available.
