What Types of Information Does Mosaic Collect and How Does Mosaic Use this Information? 

1. We collect non-personal information about online visitors in order to improve your online experience. 

To optimize your user experience and enhance your time spent with us online, we collect information about your visits to our sites, such as your IP address, Internet service provider, browser type, operating system, mobile network, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, general location information, pages accessed on our sites, the date and time of access, the content, and advertisements you have accessed, seen, forwarded and/or clicked, and the last site you visited before you came to our site. This information is collected automatically as you move through the Website(s). The information we collect automatically does not include personal information; however, we may retain or associate it with personal information that we collect in other ways or receive from third parties. We also use this information to help us improve our products, services, content, and advertising, and for loss prevention and anti-fraud purposes. The technologies used for this automated data collection may include cookies and web beacons. 

2. We collect personally identifiable information you voluntarily provide to us. 

Mosaic collects personally identifiable information, including your name, address, the name of the company for which you work, your title, your phone number, your country, and your e-mail address when you voluntarily submit this information to us. 

For example: 

  • if you click "Contact Us" at the bottom of www.cropnutrition.com, you will be invited to provide your name, the name of the company for which you work, your title, your phone number, and your country, along with your comment or question for us. We will use this information to contact you in response to your comment or question. 
  • if you use the “Retail Value Calculator”, you will provide us some commercial and personal information about your preferences (acreage, Crops, Products Sold, Products Used, Product Interest, Preferred Retailer). We may use this information to link you with your preferences.
  • if you subscribe to receive our communications, we will use your contact information to send you the Farm Journal and other content by e-mail or maybe SMS, if you prefer. 

We use this information that you provide to us, including any personal information:

  • To present our Websites and their contents to you. 
  • To provide you with information, products, or services that you request from Mosaic. 
  • To fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it. 
  • To carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection. 
  • To notify you about changes to our Website or any products or services we offer or provide through it. 
  • To help us improve our products, services, content, and advertising, and for loss prevention and anti-fraud purposes.
  • In any other way that we may describe when you provide the information. 
  • For any other purpose with your consent. 

We may also use this information, unless you request otherwise, to keep you updated about Mosaic's latest product announcements, and upcoming events.  If you do not want us to use your information in this way, please check the relevant box located on the form on which we collect your data.

Personal information will only be shared by Mosaic to provide or improve our products, services and advertising; it will not be shared with third parties for their marketing purposes.

This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for the purposes of fraud protection and credit risk reduction.

We also work with third-party companies that use their tracking technologies (including cookies and pixel tags) on our sites in order to provide tailored advertisements to you on our behalf. These companies may collect information about your activity on our Websites and your interaction with our advertising and other communications and use this information to determine which ads you see on third-party web sites and applications. You can change your cookie settings in our Website on our cookie banner. Please note that even if you opt out (clean your cookies, cache, etc.), these tracking technologies may still collect data and you will still see our ads, but they will not be targeted based upon the information collected through our tracking technology criteria.

Pages of the Websites and our e-mails may contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs) that permit Mosaic, for example, to count users who have visited those pages or opened an email and for other related Website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of certain Website content and verifying system and server integrity).
