SCOPE

This Privacy Notice applies to the collection of personal information (defined below) from our customers, vendors, business partners or any other individuals who access our services or a website/application to which this Privacy Notice is linked.

DEFINITIONS

The following definitions shall apply throughout this Privacy Notice:

"Personal Information" means information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, to you. Personal Information does not include aggregated or deidentified information that is maintained in a manner that is not reasonably capable of being associated with or linked to you. Unless the context requires otherwise, Personal Information shall be deemed to include Sensitive Personal Information.

"Sensitive Personal Information" means Personal Information that reveals genetic data or biometric data, racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious, or philosophical beliefs, trade union membership, or information specifying the health or sex life of the individual.

"Website” means Firstsolar.com and all other First Solar websites and online services, including desktop and mobile applications that post or link to this Privacy Notice.

    COOKIES
    At several points, the Website delivers so-called “cookies” (including "session" cookies that are automatically deleted upon termination of your session when you close your browser).     What do we use cookies for?
    The cookies we use are categorized into four groups:

    Strictly Necessary cookies - these cookies are used for technical reasons and are necessary to enable the Website to operate efficiently so that you can navigate the Website with ease and use specific features. These include, for example, cookies that help us to debug any errors. If these cookies are blocked or disabled, some of the Website may not operate effectively.

    Performance cookies - these cookies are used to obtain statistics about the number of users of the Website and how such users interact with the Website. These cookies collect Personal Information that is aggregated and therefore cannot be used to ascertain an individual's identity. Such Personal Information allows us to continuously improve the Website to provide users with a better online.

    Functionality cookies - these cookies are used to improve the functionality of the Website and make it easier to use.  They help us to identify you as a repeat user of the Website and help us remember your preferences (for example, your choice of language or region), improving your user experience.

    We may share Personal Information that we collected through cookies, in aggregated form, with carefully selected third parties for the purposes set forth above, unless you disable or block cookies as set forth below.

You may also set your email options to prevent the automatic downloading of images that may contain technologies. In addition, options you select relating to cookies and other tracking technologies are specific to your particular browser or device. You must exercise these choices on each browser or device you may use. Some web browsers may transmit “do-not-track” (“DNT”) signals to the websites with which the user communicates.     First Solar will only retain your Personal Information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose(s) for which we collected it, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

    Consent

    Contract

    Legal Obligation

    Legal Claim

    In all cases, in addition to the legal bases, to determine the appropriate retention period for Personal Information, we consider the amount, nature, and sensitivity of the Personal Information, the potential risk of harm from unauthorised use or disclosure of your Personal Information, the purposes for which we process your Personal Information and whether we can achieve those purposes through other means, and the applicable legal requirements.
