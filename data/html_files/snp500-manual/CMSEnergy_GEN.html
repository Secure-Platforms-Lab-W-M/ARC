We take seriously the protection of your personal information. This notice summarizes CMS Energy's information privacy policies and seeks to answer the following questions for our site’s visitors:

What information does CMS Energy collect from me?

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us (like your name and email address) as well as information about how you interact with our website (like the pages you visit).


LESS

CMS Energy collects information you voluntarily provide when you communicate with us, including:

Personal Information: Collected personal information includes your name and email address.

Online Information: We use tracking technologies, such as cookies, to collect information you provide through your online activity access and use of our websites. Examples include your Internet Protocol (IP) address -- a number that is automatically assigned to your device by your Internet Service Provider (ISP). Your IP is identified and logged automatically in our server log files whenever you visit our websites.  Your Media Access Control (MAC) address, device type, screen resolution, operating system version, browser type and version, time spent on our websites, pages you visited, location and your relationship with us, and statistical information about your use of our website.

Note: You may opt out of providing certain types of information by refraining from providing it or discontinuing your use of the website. You may also opt out of cookies by managing your cookie settings for your devices and browsers.

Aggregated/De-Identified Information: We also collect aggregated and de-identified information from various sources. Such information does not identify individuals but may be used for our business purposes including to identify new offerings, communications, researching or analyzing market trends, and other purposes consistent with applicable laws.

We use your information to provide you with the services you request, to provide customer service, and to improve website customer experience.


LESS

CMS Energy may share contact or personal information provided by you for legitimate business purposes such as providing personalized and optimal customer service, conducting business operations, complying with relevant laws, regulations, or legal processes, protecting the security of critical infrastructure and key resources, and ensuring information security.

Such information may be made available to third parties including service providers, contractors, regulators, and governmental authorities to protect the security of critical infrastructure and key resources, ensure information security, or comply with any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.

Your information may also be used to:

  • Respond to your requests and communicate with you.

    • Our websites use electronic forms for customers to request information, products, and services. Your contact information and responses are used to respond to customer requests.
  • Improve our websites by customizing page layout and content, by analyzing the statistical information that we collect about your use of our websites to enable us to improve design and functionality, monitor responses to our communications and content, and to help us resolve customer questions regarding our websites.         
    • IP Address: We collect and use your IP address to personalize your experience online and offline, and to maintain the full functionality of our websites, including calculating site usage, diagnosing server problems, compliance, and security purposes.  Collecting IP addresses is standard practice on the internet and is done automatically by many sites.
    • Cookies: We use cookie information for security purposes, to facilitate navigation, to display information more effectively, to customize your experience and to recognize your device to allow for an easy and convenient use of our online services. Cookies may also be used for communications purposes. We use cookies on your computer and portable devices to help us better understand your preferences so we can customize our content to best meet your needs. Cookies help us provide you with a continuous and more personalized experience.
    • Browser and Device: Information collected by most browsers and/or through your device, such as your Media Access Control (MAC) address, device type, screen resolution, operating system version and internet browser type and version, is used to ensure our sites function properly, for fraud detection and prevention, and security purposes.

How does CMS Energy protect my information?

We protect your information using industry-standard technologies such as device encryption, physical safeguards such as locked devices and buildings, and procedural safeguards such as restrictions on who can access what data.


LESS

We have rigorous security measures in place to protect and prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information that you provide us. These measures include device safeguards, industry-standard encryption technologies, and secured files and buildings.

To protect your information from unauthorized access and use, we use security measures that comply with applicable federal and state laws, as well as governmental and quasi-governmental agencies with regulatory authority. We protect the confidentiality of the personal information we collect by maintaining physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards, with additional security measures and precautions for any highly sensitive personal information.

The measures we use to safeguard your information include:

  • Information technology and physical security measures.  
  • Limiting access to personal information.
  • Prohibiting unlawful disclosure of personal information.
  • Training our employees in the proper handling of personal information.
  • Requiring that third parties that receive or have access to personal information, such as service providers and contractors, comply with all applicable laws and secure, keep confidential, and limit access to and use of your personal information.
  • Reviewing our safeguards regularly.


Questions regarding this policy or requests to correct your personal information can be directed to privacy@cmsenergy.com or by calling 1-800-477-5050.
