Hess Corporation is committed to respecting and maintaining the privacy of our customers and the visitors to this site. Please read this Privacy Policy so that you can gain an understanding of what data we collect, how we use it and the safeguards we have in place to protect any personal information which you may provide to us through your use of this site.

In general, when you access this site, no personal information about you is collected. If you request information from us, your email address will be recorded in order to provide you with the information which you have requested. This information will be used only to process your information request or to respond to your inquiry. WE DO NOT SELL OR DISTRIBUTE TO THIRD PARTIES ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION COLLECTED VIA THIS SITE. When you access this site we automatically collect certain non-personal information such as the type of internet browser you are using or the website from which you have accessed this site. You cannot be identified from this information and it is used solely to enable us to provide this site to you in the most efficient manner possible.

We may store some information on your computer when you access this site. This information is commonly known as a "cookie" and once recorded is not accessible by any other company. We use cookies in order to better understand how the site is being accessed and used and cookies also help us to determine if the site is being delivered efficiently. In addition, cookies allow us to enhance your on line experience at Hess.com by customizing your use of the site and eliminating the need for you to reenter data every time you visit the site. You should be able to block these cookies in the first instance or erase them from your computer after they have been recorded, if you so choose. Please consult your computer manual in this regard. If you block or erase these cookies you may limit the functionality available to you on our site.

Hess Corporation uses security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the information and personal data that we have under our control from access by unauthorized users, improper disclosure, modification, alteration or destruction.

Hess Corporation reserves the right to modify, change or amend these policies without notice and to gather additional information about our visitors, such as when additional functionality on the site demands it. For example, if products are offered for sale or other financial transactions added to the site, additional information will be required. Hess Corporation will take all reasonable steps necessary to protect whatever personal information is gathered.

If you are under 18 years of age, please read these Privacy Policies with your parents and ask questions about things you do not understand. Children under age 13 should get the permission of a parent or guardian before providing Hess Corporation - or anyone else - with an email address or any other personal information. Hess Corporation does not intend to collect personal information from or about persons under the age of 18.
