California and Virginia Privacy Policy

In this notice, we inform TEs consumers who are based in California and Virginia about their privacy rights as protected by applicable laws such as the CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act) and VCDPA (Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act).

 

For the collection and processing of your personal information TEs website privacy policy and TEs cookie policy apply as well unless this Notice provides different rights.

TE collects the following types of information

When visiting our websites, we will collect your Internet Protocol (IP) address as well as other information, such as browser, device type, domain, host names, pages viewed and viewing patterns, as well as the country/region where you are located.

 

Once you complete an account on our website or another webform we can collect your contact details including name, email address, physical address, company name, function and phone number, as well as your industry of interest. When you place an order in the TE Store we also collect information relating to your purchase.

Purposes for which we use your personal information

The information collected when visiting our website allows us to better determine user needs and interests to drive improvements to website content and functionality, and to improve the overall customer service experience.

 

We use the information you provide in the account or web forms for analyzing and improving our website, for sending you (upon request) samples of TE products, for sending you direct marketing information and for executing your order in the TE Store.

We can also use your personal information for the additional purposes as stated
in TEs website privacy policy:

  • Website Registration
  • Request for Information Regarding Product/Service
  • Website Features and Updates
  • Product/Service Enhancements and Information
  • Webinar/Training Registration
  • Survey Participation
  • Live Chat Support and use of Chatbot
  • Customer Service Support
  • Job Applications
  • Product Information Center Support
  • Sample Requests
  • Sales Transactions and Support
  • Operate the TE Store
  • Export Controls
  • Participation in Public Forums
  • Social Media Advertising
  • Sweepstakes Administration
  • Customize Account Preferences
  • Marketing Purposes
  • Enabling the download and use of a TE mobile app and the various functions it provides which may include the use of the camera of your device or collecting its location.

Selling or sharing of personal information

TE does not sell your personal information as such for monetary consideration. Still, we may provide personal information to TE distributors and certain business partners for sales and marketing purposes. We may also provide third parties personal information for behavioral marketing. When you dont want this, you can click on the following link: Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information. You can also call the following toll-free number 1-866-707-7754.

Invoke your privacy rights

Under applicable laws you can ask us for the following information by completing the webform or by calling the following toll-free number 1-866-707-7754: 

  • Right to know about personal information that we collected, disclosed or sold
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for the exercise of a consumers privacy rights
  • Right to have your personal information corrected

 

You can also ask another person to invoke these rights on your behalf as your authorized agent by completing the webform.

 

 For any additional questions you can email: privacyoffice@te.com
