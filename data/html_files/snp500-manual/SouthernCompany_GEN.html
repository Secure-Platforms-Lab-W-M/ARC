Please review those terms carefully, as such terms may differ from the terms of this general Privacy Statement.

Please review this Privacy Statement regularly for any changes or updates. We may change this Privacy Statement at any time and with notice to you on the Web Site. Information We Collect When You Visit the Web Sites

Except as set forth in this Privacy Statement, we do not collect personal information on the Web Sites unless you voluntarily provide it to us when you visit the Web Sites. When you visit them, our Web Sites automatically collect certain information about interactions between your web browser and the Web Sites and other web sites unrelated to the Web Sites that you visit, including your IP address, the web pages you visit and how long you visit them. Web browsers may provide certain information that we may also collect, including your time zone, browser type and version and language preference. We or our third party service providers may organize or analyze IP addresses for general support of internal operations, including maintaining functionality of the Web Sites and diagnosing Web Sites' issues, analyzing trends, providing more efficient service, maintaining and/or enhancing security for users and the Web Sites, producing traffic volume statistics and making the Web Sites and our infrastructure more useful and secure. In an effort to protect the security of our critical infrastructure, any use of a Web Site or information relating to such use may be monitored and disclosed to federal authorities or agencies.

The Web Site located at www.southerncompany.com may also share certain information such as cookie data (as discussed more specifically below) with third-party advertising partners. This information would enable you to view advertisements that they believe will be of interest to you.

Cookies

Some parts of the Web Sites use "cookies." Our Web Sites may ask your web browser to store a small text file on your computer, which will then be sent back to our Web Sites automatically each time you click on a new page on the Web Sites. This enables us to provide you with the best browsing experience possible. A cookie cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses. You can refuse the cookie or delete the cookie file from your computer by adjusting your web browser privacy settings or by other widely available methods. Please note that you may not be able to use certain features of the Web Sites if your web browser does not accept cookies.

When you visit the Web Sites, we may ask if you would like us to send you marketing or customer service materials about our services and other associated products and services. As part of our efforts to provide you with better products and services and to increase customer satisfaction, we may share information with third-party providers that assist us in providing those products and/or services to you. From time to time, we may include survey forms on the Web Sites. For example, we may survey customers concerning issues that are important to us, such as customer opinions or services offered. We may disclose aggregated survey results to other third parties, such as the public service commission, rating agencies, or advertising agencies. Additionally, we do provide our survey vendors with limited personal information needed to conduct the survey, such as names, email addresses or telephone numbers.
