Information we collect from our website. We may also collect certain information about you when you visit our website. If you access our website via a link from a different website, for example, we may collect the URL of that website. Similarly, if you access a different website via a link from our website, we may collect the URL of that website as well. We may additionally collect information about your operating system, your browser type, your IP address, the general area in which your server is located, and the type of the mobile device you use to access the website. We and our vendors further collect information concerning how you use our website, including when you visit it, how much time you spend viewing it, the parts that you visit, the search terms that you enter, transaction data, and other click-stream data. If you download materials from our website, we may also collect information about those files.

We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" ("DNT") signals sent by web browsers because a uniform technological standard has not yet been developed.

Cookies and pixels. We and our vendors use cookies, web beacons, pixels (e.g., through a Facebook pixel), or similar technologies to enable basic features of our website, to collect data about your use of our website and optimize your experience, for analytics purposes, to display advertising for our products and services that is relevant to you online or by email, to measure the performance of our advertising, and for recruiting potential employees. We and our vendors also use pixels and cookies in our emails to you so that we and our email vendors can record open rates, evaluate the effectiveness of our email campaigns, and engage in further marketing to those who display an interest in our products or services.

A cookie is a small data text file that a website stores on your hard drive (if your browser permits) that can help identify you when you return to the same website. The cookies track what parts of our website you visit so that we may improve the information and functionality of our website.

It is your choice whether cookies are stored on your computer or device and whether to delete them. By accepting cookies that are stored on your computer when you visit our website, you consent to the use of cookies as outlined in this Policy. You can set your browser to reject cookies, but if you do, it may affect certain features or services of our website. Google Analytics. Google Analytics is a service provided by Google, Inc. ("Google"). We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized statistics in order to improve the website. You can learn about Google's practices by going to Google Analytics and privacy, and opt out of them by downloading the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on(opens in a new tab). We have taken the following measures to protect the data collected through Google Analytics:

  • We have concluded a data processing agreement with Google about how Google may process the data collected through Google Analytics;
  • We have configured Google Analytics settings to mask the last octet of user IP addresses;

  • We do not use other Google services in conjunction with Google Analytics.

In addition to Google Analytics, we also use Sitecore analytics. By using Sitecore analytics, we can track user engagement and behavior and are able to provide the most relevant content and enhance the user experience. You may opt out by sending a request to webworks@skyworksinc.com to remove your profile.



3. Why do we collect the information?

Our primary goal in collecting Personal Information is to provide our visitors with superior service and a customized online experience. We collect and process the information with consent and/or as necessary to provide the products or services you use, operate our business and website, and fulfill other legitimate interests. More specifically, the purposes for which we collect and use Personal Information include:

  • Providing you with the information, subscriptions, products, and services you request;
  • Allowing you to establish and maintain your account, confirm the details and statuses of your orders, and make returns;

  • Providing you with information and advertisements concerning our product or service offerings, as well as transactional or other communications, by email, online and mobile advertising, social media, and other channels, including personalizing advertisements across website and mobile applications or analyzing or measuring advertisements based on information associated with your device;
