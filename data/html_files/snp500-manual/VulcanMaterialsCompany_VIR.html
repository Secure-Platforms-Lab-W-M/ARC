VIRGINIA PRIVACY POLICY


Effective Date: January 1, 2023
Last Revised on: December 21, 2022


This Virginia Privacy Policy (“Virginia Disclosure”) explains how Vulcan Materials Company and its affiliates and related entities and its subsidiaries and affiliates (“Company”, “us”, “ our”, or “we”) collect, use, and disclose personal information subject to the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act. This Virginia Disclosure applies solely to Virginia residents (“consumers” or “you”) and supplements our Privacy Policy.

 

This Virginia Disclosure does not apply to information we collect in the context of a Virginia resident acting in their commercial or employment context.


THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU

 

We may collect the following categories of personal information about you:

 

  • Identifiers: Name, email address, home address, phone number, and any other information you provide to us directly (e.g., conducting search queries or reporting a problem).
  • Commercial Information: Details of your transactions or purchases with us.
  • Financial Information: Such as payment card information when you make a purchase with us.
  • Internet or Other Similar Online Activity & Identifiers: Including website usage data, device data, internet connection, IP address, operating system, and browser type.


THE PURPOSES FOR COLLECTING INFORMATION ABOUT YOU

 

We use the above personal information we collect about you for the following purposes:

 

  • To provide our Services to you.
  • To improve our Services.
  • To estimate our audience size and usage patterns.
  • To store information about your preferences, allowing us to customize our Website according to your individual interests.
  • To speed up your searches.
  • To recognize you when you return to our Website.
  • To provide you with information, products, or services that you request from us.
  • To provide you with notices about your account/subscription, including expiration and renewal notices.
  • To carry out our obligations and enforce our rights arising from any contracts entered into between you and us, including for billing and collection.
  • To notify you about changes to our Services or any products or services we offer or provide through them.
  • To conduct hiring.
  • To allow you to view tickets and order details.
  • To allow you to track your orders.
  • To allow you to view your invoices and pay your bills online.
  • To allow you to receive email invoices.
  • To provide you with support.
  • In any other way we describe when you provide the information.
  • To fulfill any other purpose for which you provide it or with your consent.

YOUR VIRGINIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

 

Virginia law provides Virginia residents with the rights listed below. To exercise these rights, see the “Exercising Your Virginia Privacy Rights” section below.

 

Right to Access: You have the right to confirm whether we process certain personal information and to request access to that personal information.

 

Right to Delete: You have the right to request that we delete the personal information we have collected about you.

 

Right to Correct: You have the right to correct inaccuracies in your personal information that we have collected, taking into account the nature of the personal information and the purposes of the processing of your personal information.

 

Right to Portability: You have the right to obtain a copy of your personal information we have collected in a readily usable and transferable format in order for you to transmit the information to another business. You may only exercise this right for the personal information we collect through automated means.

 

Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell your personal information, conduct targeted advertising, or conduct any profiling. Therefore, we do not respond to requests to opt-out.

 

EXERCISING YOUR VIRGINIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

 

To exercise any of your Virginia privacy rights, please submit a request by clicking here to access our webform and inputting the requested information or by calling us at 1-800-873-4898, where we will request the same information from you listed in our webform.

 

Once we receive your request, we will verify the information you provided by matching the information that we have collected. If we cannot authenticate your request, we may ask for additional information from you. If you are unable to provide additional information, or we are unable to authenticate the request using commercially reasonable efforts, we may deny your request. If we are able to authenticate the request, we will respond to your request within 45 calendar days.

 

APPEAL PROCESS

 

If we deny a request from you, you may appeal that decision to us within 7 days of receiving our response by contacting us at 1-800-873-4893 or clicking here .

 

After we receive your appeal request, we will respond to your appeal within 60 calendar days.
