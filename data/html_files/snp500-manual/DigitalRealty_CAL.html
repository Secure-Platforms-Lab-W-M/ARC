    Data Requests | Data Processing Agreement | Digital Realty Sub-Processor List | Cookie Notice | California Residents

    If you are a California resident, as defined under California law, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018 ("the CCPA") regarding the personal information that businesses have collected about you. This includes the right to request to know the personal information we store about you or deletion of your personal information in certain circumstances, as well as the right to direct a business to stop selling your personal information. Digital Realty does not sell your personal information in exchange for any monetary consideration, however we do share your personal information for other purposes that could be deemed a “sale” as defined by the CCPA. Please be aware there may be circumstances where we can share information with others even if you instruct us to stop "selling" your personal information, as permitted under the CCPA.

    You can find the categories of personal information that we collect, the sources from which we may collect your personal information, and the business purposes for such data collection in our Privacy Practices. Our Privacy Practices also cover additional disclosures about your personal information that the CCPA requires that we provide to you.

You can manage your choices by clicking on “Cookie Settings” link above to allow or prevent such uses. Depending on your choices, during the past twelve months, we may have “sold” to authorized partners information in the following categories as defined by the CCPA: identifiers like IP addresses and cookies, internet activity information relating to your interactions with our websites, and inferences about your preferences.
