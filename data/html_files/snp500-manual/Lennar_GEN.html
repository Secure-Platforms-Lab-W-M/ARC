Personal Information We Collect

What is Personal Information?

We use the term “personal information” in this Privacy Policy to refer to information that identifies, relates to, describes, or is reasonably capable of being associated or linked, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with you (and your household if you are a resident of California). This Privacy Policy does not cover information that is publicly available, aggregated or anonymized, collected by social media or third-party websites you reach by navigating from links on our Site, or information about Lennar personnel.

Categories of Information We Collect

The categories of personal information we collected and used for our business and commercial purposes during the past 12 months include the following: personal identifiers (e.g., name, e-mail and postal address, government-issued identification numbers and account password); consumer information (e.g., telephone number, credit card and bank account information); commercial activity (e.g., credit reports, Lennar and Lennar Affiliate transaction history); location; device and internet activity including your device identifiers and internet browsing history (“Usage Information”). We may also obtain professional and employment-related information, educational information and personal characteristics. We maintain customer profile records that combine information from various sources about, for example, your transactions, home needs, preferences, interests and experiences with us.

Specific Types of Personal Information You May Provide

  • Home purchases, leases and other services you request. If you submit an online or other request to receive information about our products and services, we will collect your contact information including name, address, email address, telephone number, locations and Lennar communities of interest to you and other information we need to fulfill your request.

  • myLennar account. To create a myLennar Account, we request and collect your name, email address, new account password, phone number, state and county of residence and state where you are looking for a home. When you are ready to move forward with the purchase or lease of a home, we will ask you to submit an application and enter into contracts containing identification, financial and other personal information. If you apply for a mortgage, title insurance, or other insurance, similar information may be collected by and shared with a Lennar Affiliate. Some transactions require you to provide certain financial and other personal information, including Sensitive Information, pertinent to providing the specific services, such as your signature, social security number, driver’s license, passport number and information about income, assets and liabilities.   • Credit card and bank account information. If you enter into a contract to buy, lease or finance a home or for other services, your credit card, debit card or bank account information will be processed by a third-party payment processor. Your card information is not stored by Lennar.

  • Friends and family. You may provide the contact information for friends and family to receive a notification regarding your move or for other stated purposes.

  • Job applicant information. When you make inquiries concerning career opportunities or apply for a position as a Lennar associate, we will request your name, contact information, copy of your resume, education and skills background and employment history.

  • Other information you provide. In the course of doing business with Lennar, you may choose to provide information about your employment, education, family, gender, home location and design preferences and other personal matters.

When we request Sensitive Information, we and any Lennar Affiliate we share it with will use Sensitive Information only for purposes we reasonably believe you would expect based on the products or services you request, your interactions with us, or as authorized by law. Unless we request Sensitive Information for a specific purpose (for example to obtain credit information for a home purchase or in connection with special accommodations for your home design), we ask that you not send or disclose any Sensitive Information including financial and medical information, to Lennar.

How We Use Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

When you use our Sites, we and our third-party data analytics and advertising partners may collect and receive Usage Information from cookies and other internet tracking technologies (together, “cookies”) placed on your device or browser. You can restrict some types of cookies from being placed on your device or browser as indicated below in the section called “How to Restrict Cookies.” Cookies support a wide range of website operations including the following:

Strictly necessary cookies are required for the basic operation and performance of our Sites. These are session cookies that are erased when you close your browser.

Functional/Performance cookies are used to identify and verify you and your device when you log in to access our Sites by storing, for example, your password, device identifier, browser type, operating system, Internet Service Provider (ISP) and Internet Protocol (IP) addresses. These cookies may also enable other features and options you select to operate, recognize and implement your preferences and enable information sharing with third-party providers. These cookies qualify as persistent cookies because they remain on your device or browser for the next time you access the Sites. You can restrict or delete these cookies as explained below.

Analytics Cookies let us know, for example, how you and other visitors interact with our Sites and which other websites you browsed before and after visiting our Sites. We also use this data for statistics about how many people are visiting our Sites, whether visitors are new or repeat visitors, which pages visitors are viewing and for how long and other data about usage of our Sites. These cookies qualify as persistent cookies because they remain on your device or browser after you close the session. You can restrict or delete these cookies as explained below. We may obtain usage and activity data from Lennar Affiliates that operate separate websites and from third parties that host or manage certain features of our Online Services. Each of these companies has its own policies on the collection and use of data collected using cookies and other internet technologies.

Advertising Cookies may be placed on your device or browser by us or our advertising partners including Google Ads and Facebook Ads. We and our advertising partners may use these cookies to build a profile of your interests and show you advertisements on websites, apps and platforms operated by others. Advertising cookies do not store directly identifying personal information, but store data that may uniquely identify your browser and device. You can restrict or delete these cookies as explained below.

E-mail Cookies may be used to give us information such as how you interact with the e-mail, when and whether you opened it, what links you click within the e-mail and if it was forwarded.

Third-Party Features on our Sites are powered by third-party providers that may use cookies in providing these features and functionality.
