The types of personal information we may collect directly from you include:

  • Basic User Information: such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, company or employer name, job title or position, username or user ID;

COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We and some of our Service Providers use cookies or other tracking technologies (such as web beacons and tracking pixels) to collect and store information about your interactions with our websites, including information about your browsing behavior. A cookie is a small file that is placed on your device when you visit a website. The information the cookie contains can be used by the website server to recognize you whenever you use your device to return to the website. The information collected by cookies and other technologies includes IP addresses, browser characteristics, device IDs and characteristics, operating system versions, language preferences, dates and times of your access to the website, referring URLs, email receipts and opens, and information about your usage of the website.

We use cookies to ensure that our websites function properly, to personalize content and ads, to provide social media features, to analyze website traffic, and to evaluate and improve our websites. We may also share information about your use of our websites with our social media, advertising and analytics partners who may combine it with other information about you to provide us with insights about you.

Cookies on our websites are either first-party or third-party cookies. First-party cookies are set by us and share the same domain as our websites. Third-party cookies are stored by third-party providers and organizations with different domains. Some cookies expire once you close your browser (called session cookies), and other cookies stay on your device until you delete them or they expire (called persistent cookies).

We use the following categories of cookies on our websites:

  • Necessary: These cookies allow us to operate certain features of our websitesfor example, allowing you to log in.
  • Functionality: These cookies optimize the functionality of our websites, such as by personalizing content for you, greeting you by name and remembering your preferences (e.g., your choice of language or region).
  • Performance/Analytics: These cookies collect data about how you use our websites to measure and report statistics about user interactions and to improve how our websites work. More information on website analytics is provided below.
  • Advertising: These cookies are used to deliver digital advertisements relevant to you or your interests, including retargeting on social media platforms or other areas of the Internet. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and to help measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. Information collected through advertising cookies may be shared with other organizations (e.g., advertisers).
  • Content: These cookies help display content, such as a feed from a social media platform.
