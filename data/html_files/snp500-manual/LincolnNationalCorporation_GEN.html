Online and Mobile Privacy Notice

The Lincoln Financial Group companies* (collectively, “Lincoln”) value and respect your privacy. Lincoln has established this Online and Mobile Privacy Notice ("Notice") to describe how we manage the personal information we collect online from visitors to our Lincoln websites and mobile applications (“Apps”) (collectively, our “Sites”). This Notice only applies to the information we collect about individuals who visit our Sites.

If you have another relationship with us (for example, if you are a Lincoln accountholder), the privacy notice applicable to that relationship can be found here: Privacy. If you reside in California, our California Privacy Notice supplements this Notice. You can find it here: California Privacy Notice.

Online collection of personal information

Generally. We may collect personal information directly from you if you interact with our Sites, such as if you apply for online account access, submit an inquiry to us, complete an online form, or participate in a survey. The scope of the personal information that we collect from you will vary depending on the nature of your interaction with our Sites, but may include your name, address, date of birth, telephone number, email address, and policy number.

Online technologies. Lincoln and selected third parties may use cookies and other online technologies to collect information such as your internet browser type, IP address, device ID, operating system version, location, and demographics, as well as the areas of the Sites you visit, what content you interact with, and the time of your visit.

We may combine certain automatically collected information with other personal information we obtain about you, such as data we obtain from third parties. We or external parties may collect personal information about your online activities over time and across different online services when you use the Sites.

The online technologies (collectively, “cookies”) Lincoln utilizes can be categorized as:

Required cookies: These cookies are necessary for the Sites to function.

Functional cookies: These cookies allow us to analyze use of the Sites to evaluate and improve performance and may include session replay technologies. Functional cookies may also be used to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

Advertising cookies: Lincoln uses these cookies to show you personalized ads by collecting personal information such as your IP address, digital identifiers, and your interactions with our websites and other media. We may share this information with our advertising partners, who may use this information for their own purposes.

Third parties. We may engage third parties to track and analyze data about our Sites. We use that information to help us administer and improve the quality of our Sites and to analyze usage data. These third parties may combine the information that we provide about you with other information that they have collected. This Policy does not cover such third parties' use of the data. These third parties may include web analytics services, including Google, Facebook, and others, to help us understand and analyze how you interact with the Sites and to serve ads on our behalf across the internet. We use automated devices and applications to evaluate usage of our Sites, and to assist us in determining how to improve our Sites.

We use Display & Video 360, Campaign Manager, and Search Ads 360. We also use third party ad network providers, advertisers, sponsors and/or traffic measurement services that may use cookies, JavaScript, web beacons (including clear GIFs), Flash LSOs and other technologies to measure the effectiveness of their ads and to personalize advertising content to you. These third-party cookies and other technologies are governed by each third party's specific privacy policy, not this one. We may provide these third-party advertisers with information about your usage of our Sites, as well as aggregate or non-personal information about users of our Sites. These third parties may collect certain information, such as what website a visitor came from, your IP address, and what type of browser you are using.

Opting out

You may opt out of certain interest-based advertising using the settings on your browser or mobile device. You may set your web browser’s “do not track” or other similar browser-level or device-level mechanism, to signal to our Sites a request to disable certain cookies on our Sites. Refer to the browser or device mechanism for more information on how the mechanism interacts with different types of cookies, and note that by disabling certain types of cookies, some features on our Sites may no longer function. Also note that we do not control these browser-level or device-level opt out mechanisms and therefore our Sites will adhere to such signals to the extent reasonably technologically viable.

If you use a mobile device, you can use mobile device settings to limit mobile tracking technologies and associated activities. For instance, you can adjust or reset the advertising identifiers on your mobile device in the device settings. iOS users can visit Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Reset Advertising Identifier. Android users can visit Google settings > Ads > Reset advertising ID.

In addition, the following services or organizations offer tools that you can use to limit or opt out of certain cookies:

  • For more information on how Google Analytics uses data it collects, visit google.com/policies/privacy/partners/. To opt out of Google Analytics, visit tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. To adjust your Google advertising settings, visit adssettings.google.com.

  • We use Yahoo to monitor our website to report web traffic, statistics, advertisement ‘click-throughs,’ or other activities. Yahoo may use cookies, web beacons, or other monitoring technologies to compile anonymous statistics about our website visitors. To opt out of Yahoo’s collection of such information outside of the Yahoo distribution network, visit legal.yahoo.com/us/en/yahoo/privacy/index.html.

  • To learn more about opting out of data collection by Adobe, visit adobe.com/privacy/opt-out.html.

  • To learn how MRP Prelytix processes personal information and to manage your privacy preferences, visit www.mrpfd.com/privacy/.

  • To change your privacy and advertising settings with Facebook, log in to your Facebook account and navigate to your account settings.

  • To learn more about opting out of data collection by Oracle, visit datacloudoptout.oracle.com/optout.

  • We may use cookies to show you personalized advertisements on our Sites or on external party sites. Certain external parties may participate in the Digital Advertising Alliance’s YourAdChoices Program, which is an opt-out program for website browsers. You can learn more at www.youradchoices.com for websites and www.aboutads.info/appchoices for mobile apps. Certain advertising networks and exchanges may also participate in the Network Advertising Initiative (“NAI”). To learn more about opting out of such targeted advertising or to use the NAI tool, see www.networkadvertising.org/choices for websites and www.networkadvertising.org/mobile-choice/ for mobile apps. Please be aware that you will continue to receive non-targeted advertisements even if you are able to opt out of certain kinds of interest-based ads.

We are not responsible for the effectiveness of external-parties’ opt-out options or programs, their compliance with such options or programs, or the accuracy of their statements regarding their programs.

External party sites

Our Sites may include links to external party sites or other external party content that Lincoln does not own or control. External party sites and content may handle the collection, use or disclosure of your personal information differently than Lincoln; this Privacy Notice does not apply to those external party sites or content. A link to or from an external site or content is not an endorsement by us of the external party site, its content, its privacy practices, or the external party itself, and we are not responsible for the practices, content or accuracy of external sites or content.

Changes to our online and mobile privacy notice

We reserve the right to change this Notice. If we change it, we will post the current Notice on this page. Any changes to this Notice will be effective as of the day Lincoln posts them.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Notice, please contact privacy@lfg.com.
