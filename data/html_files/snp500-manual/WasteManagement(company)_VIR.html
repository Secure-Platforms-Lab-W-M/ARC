Privacy Notice for Virginia Residents

Effective: January 1, 2023

Waste Management Holdings, Inc. and its wholly–owned subsidiaries ("WM", "we", "our" or "us") have created this privacy notice in order to demonstrate our firm commitment to your privacy. This privacy notice applies to Personal Data of Virginia residents to the extent the information is subject to the Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA). This privacy notice does not apply to Personal Data collected (i) pursuant to the federal Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act and its implementing regulations, (ii) pursuant to certain medical and health information covered by the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (“HIPAA”) or (iii) from an individual in an employment or commercial context.

Your Privacy Rights

Submitting Consumer Requests

For details about how to submit a consumer request, you can visit our privacy portal here, or call ‎855-782-6445‎ to make a consumer request.

Only you, or someone legally authorized to act on your behalf, may make a consumer request related to your Personal Data. You may also make a consumer request on behalf of your minor child.

Making a consumer request does not require you to create an account with us.

When you submit a consumer request, we will take steps to verify your request ‎by email or telephone call. In some cases, we may request additional information in order to verify ‎your request or where necessary to process your request. If we are unable to adequately verify a ‎request, we will notify the requestor. Authorized agents will be required to provide proof of their ‎authorization and we may also require that the relevant consumer directly verify their identity and ‎the authority of the authorized agent.‎

We will only use Personal Data provided in a consumer request to verify the requestor's identity or authority to make the request.

We cannot respond to your request or provide you with Personal Data if we cannot verify your identity or authority to make the request and confirm the Personal Data relates to you.

Right to Appeal

You have the right to appeal our responses to your consumer requests. If you believe you have a basis for such an appeal, please contact us here: you can visit our privacy portal here, or call ‎ 855-782-6445‎ to make an appeal request and provide any required information to submit your appeal.

Right to Know and Access

You have the right to request that we confirm whether or not we are processing your Personal Data and to request access to that Personal Data. To submit this request please review the section above titled, “Submitting Consumer Requests.”

Right to Obtain a Copy of Personal Data

You have the right to obtain a copy of your Personal Data that you previously provided to us in a portable and, to the extent technically feasible, readily usable format that allows you to transmit the data to another controller without hindrance, where the processing is carried out by automated means. To submit this request please review the section above titled, “Submitting Consumer Requests.”

Right to Request Deletion

You have the right to request that we delete Personal Data about you that we process. You may make such a request by visiting our privacy portal and through other means. See “Submitting Consumer Requests” above for additional details.

You should be aware, however, that Virginia law allows us to retain your Personal Data under certain conditions, even if you have asked us to delete it, such as when retaining your Personal Data is necessary for us to complete a transaction with you or provide you with a good or service you have requested. We will notify you of any denial of your deletion request and the reason for such denial.

Right to Request Correction

You have the right to request that we correct inaccuracies in your Personal Data, taking into account the nature of the Personal Data and the purposes of the processing of your Personal Data. See “Submitting Consumer Requests” above for additional details about how to submit a request.

Right to opt out of certain processing

Consumers have the right to opt out of certain categories of processing of Personal Data. These include the right to opt out for purposes of (i) targeted advertising, (ii) the sale of Personal Data, or (iii) profiling in furtherance of decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects concerning the consumer. We do not process Personal Data for these purposes and therefore these opt out rights do not apply.
