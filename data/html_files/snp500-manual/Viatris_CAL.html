                                                                            Supplemental Disclosure for California Residents

                                                                            Last Updated: September 2020

                                                                            This Supplemental Disclosure for California Residents (“Disclosure”) supplements our Global Privacy Notice (“Notice”) by providing additional information about how Viatris, Inc. and its affiliates and subsidiaries (collectively, “Viatris,” “Company,” “we,” “our,” and “us”) process Personal Data relating to California residents, unless such processing occurs solely outside of California. We strongly encourage you to read in full our Notice in addition to this Disclosure.

                                                                            In the event of a conflict between the Notice and this Disclosure, the Disclosure shall control with respect to our processing of Personal Data relating to California residents (unless such processing occurs solely outside of California). Otherwise the Notice shall control.

                                                                            The Disclosure does not describe how we process Personal Data on behalf of other businesses, in which case we would act as a “service provider” under California law. The Disclosure also does not describe how we process de-identified or aggregated data, as those terms are defined under California law.

                                                                               2. Purposes of Processing Personal Data

                                                                            Please see the section of the Notice entitled “Why We Process Personal Data” for more information about the business and commercial purposes for which we collect, use, and disclose Personal Data, including Personal Data relating to California residents.

                                                                               3. Personal Data Disclosure

                                                                            Please see the section of the Notice entitled “Disclosures of Personal Data” for more information about the circumstances in which we disclose Personal Data and the parties to whom we disclose such Personal Data.

                                                                            Please see the section of the Notice entitled “Your Choices and Rights” for more information about the rights to which you might be entitled.

We will not unlawfully deny you goods or services for exercising your privacy rights. To exercise your right to opt-out of sales, please click this button: Do Not Sell My Personal Information.

                                                                            If you are submitting a request through an authorized agent, the authorized agent must provide documentation proving they have your permission. We may also request that authorized agents independently verify their identity, and we may ask you directly to confirm your identify and that you have authorized the agent to submit the request on your behalf.

                                                                            We may update this Disclosure from time to time. You are encouraged to consult this Disclosure on a regular basis and check the date on which it was last updated, as shown in the beginning of the Disclosure. When we make a material change to the Disclosure, we will endeavor to notify you by placing a notice on our website or through other notifications as otherwise may be required by law.
